Purpose of this Archive

Welcome to the official record-bearing platform for Solomon Kin Records. This site has been established to serve as the definitive ledger for our complex ecosystem of for-profit companies, nonprofit organizations, and charitable foundations.

Our Role: We are a company record-keeping and management firm. We specialize in the administration of people, the oversight of corporate affiliations, and the documentation of inter-company contractual obligations.

Scope of Documentation

This site replaces previous web iterations to focus strictly on data integrity and historical accuracy. We document:

  • Corporate Affiliations: Mapping the relationships between all entities within the Solomon Kin umbrella.
  • Inter-Company Contracts: Recording the specific instances of collaboration and service agreements between our internal organizations.
  • Nonprofit Oversight: Managing the records and foundational filings for our community-focused entities.
  • Management Records: Centralizing the data regarding the personnel and leadership governing these affiliations.

As a community organization of affiliations, this site ensures that every instance of organizational activity is logged, verified, and accessible for administrative management.
